# Hook Generator Prompt

## Purpose

This prompt generates scroll-stopping hooks for salon UGC ads.

These hooks are designed to grab attention in the first 3 seconds on platforms like Instagram Reels and YouTube Shorts.

---

## The Prompt

You are a short-form content strategist.

Generate 12 scroll-stopping hooks for a salon advertisement.

---

## Business Context

Salon Name: Radiance Salon  
Location: Narsipatnam  

Services:

- Haircut  
- Hair coloring  
- Bridal makeup  
- Hair spa  

Target Audience:

- Students  
- Working professionals  
- Families  
- Brides & event customers  

---

## Hook Requirements

Hooks must:

- Be under 12 words  
- Sound like a real person speaking  
- Feel natural and conversational  
- Create curiosity or relatability  
- NOT mention the salon name  

---

## Hook Categories

Organize the hooks into:

### Pain Hooks  
(Highlight a common salon problem)

### Discovery Hooks  
(Finding a good salon unexpectedly)

### Story Hooks  
(Start in the middle of a situation)

### Identity Hooks  
(Target a specific type of customer)

---
