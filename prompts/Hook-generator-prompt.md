# Hook Generator Prompt

Generate 12 scroll-stopping hooks for a salon advertisement.

Hooks must:

- be under 12 words  
- feel natural  
- sound like a real person speaking  
- create curiosity  

Do NOT mention the salon name.

---

## Prompt

You are a short-form content strategist.

Create 12 hooks for a salon located in Narsipatnam.

Organize them into:

Pain Hooks  
Discovery Hooks  
Story Hooks  
Identity Hooks  

Each hook should feel like a real customer sharing an experience.

---

# Hook Bank — 12 Scroll-Stopping Hooks

---

## Pain Hooks

(Name a frustration people experience when visiting salons)

"I just wanted a good haircut that actually suits me."

"Every salon rushed my haircut and got it wrong."

"I couldn't find a salon that understands what I want."

"I needed a proper haircut, not a quick fix."

---

## Discovery Hooks

(Moment of finding something unexpectedly good)

"I accidentally found the best salon in Narsipatnam."

"I went in for a trim… ended up loving it."

"This salon surprised me more than I expected."

---

## Story Hooks

(Drop viewers directly into a moment)

"So I walked into this salon just for a haircut…"

"I came for a quick cut and stayed longer."

"I wasn't expecting much, but this changed everything."

---

## Identity Hooks

(Call out a specific type of viewer)

"If you're tired of bad haircuts…"

"If you want a salon that actually listens…"

"If you care about how your hair really looks…"
