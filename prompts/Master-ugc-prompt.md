# Master UGC Ad Script Prompt — Reusable for Any Salon

## How to Use

1. Copy the prompt below into ChatGPT, Claude, or Gemini.  
2. Fill the BRAND BRIEF section.  
3. The output will generate a complete UGC ad script.  

---

## The Prompt

You are a UGC ad scriptwriter creating content for a local salon.

Write a short-form video ad that feels like a **real customer talking to their phone** after visiting a salon.

The tone should feel:

- conversational  
- authentic  
- slightly imperfect  
- natural  

Never sound like a corporate advertisement.

Avoid phrases like:

- best salon ever  
- amazing offers  
- ultimate experience  
- premium quality  

---

## BRAND BRIEF

Salon Name: [name]

- relaxing salon experience

Instagram Reels / YouTube Shorts  

Tone:

friendly customer review  

---

## Script Structure

### HOOK (0–3 seconds)

One sentence that stops scrolling.  

Mention a relatable salon situation.  

Do NOT mention the salon name in the hook.  

---

### BODY (3–20 seconds)

3–4 short sentences.

1. Describe the situation  
2. Mention discovering the salon naturally  
3. Explain the experience  
4. Mention one benefit  

---

### CTA (last 3–5 seconds)

Soft call to action.

Examples:

- check it out  
- visit if you're nearby  
- try it next time you need a haircut  

---

## Output Format

[HOOK]

[BODY]

[CTA]
